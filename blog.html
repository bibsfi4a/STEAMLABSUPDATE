Space Newspaper

At every venue we hold our programs at, one of our 3 culminating activities is the "GalaxSea Explorers Space Newspaper". Students practice communicating the content curriculum and hands-on activities they've learned about by forming these ideas into their own words in an article format. Their articles are posted to this blog, and this is a fun experience for students to become published news writers!
